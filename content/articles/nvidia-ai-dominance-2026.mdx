## üèÜ The Undisputed Champion

NVIDIA's dominance in AI chips is staggering:

- **90%+ market share** in AI training chips
- **80%+ market share** in AI inference chips
- **CUDA ecosystem**: 4+ million developers locked in
- **H100/H200 GPUs**: $30,000-$40,000 per unit, selling out instantly

### The Moat

What makes NVIDIA's position so strong?

1. **Software Ecosystem (CUDA)**
   - 15+ years of developer optimization
   - Switching cost = rewriting entire AI stack
   - Competitors struggle with compatibility

2. **Performance Lead**
   - H100 is 3-5x faster than AMD's MI300
   - Next-gen Blackwell chips promise another 2x leap

3. **Supply Chain Control**
   - Exclusive partnership with TSMC
   - Priority access to cutting-edge nodes
